# Haiku Revision Checklist

## Core Checks

- Is there exactly one seasonal focus?
- Is the image concrete enough to visualize immediately?
- Is the emotional statement shown indirectly rather than explained?
- Is there one clear cut (kire) in syntax or cutting word?
- Does the line flow sound natural when read aloud?

## Compression Checks

- Remove one adjective or adverb.
- Replace one abstract word with a concrete noun.
- Remove duplicate meaning across phrases.

## Perspective Checks

- Keep tense consistent.
- Keep distance consistent (close-up vs wide scene).
- Avoid sudden speaker intrusion unless intentional.

## Comparison Rubric for Candidate Verses

Score each candidate 1-5 on:

- Seasonal clarity
- Visual sharpness
- Resonance after reading
- Linguistic freshness
- Read-aloud rhythm

Select the verse with the highest total, then do one final reduction pass.
