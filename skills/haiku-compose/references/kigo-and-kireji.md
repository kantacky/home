# Seasonal Words and Kireji Quick Reference

## How to Use

- Pick one seasonal anchor first.
- Prefer concrete nouns over explanatory phrases.
- If the seasonal word is strong, remove extra seasonal explanation.

## Kireji (Cutting Words) and Effects

- や: Open a scene and leave resonance.
- かな: Gentle emotional closure.
- けり: Discovery or realization at the end.

## Structural Cuts Without Kireji

- Use a noun break: noun + noun juxtaposition.
- Use time shift: present scene to slight hindsight.
- Use perspective shift: object detail to observer response.
